Voice2Action
Voice2Action is an application designed to streamline productivity by converting voice commands into actionable tasks and meeting events.
Features
Voice-to-Action: Capture meeting details, task deadlines, and general notes using voice input.
Task Management: Automatically creates and organizes tasks based on your voice commands.
Meeting Organization: Extracts meeting times and titles to keep your schedule organized.
How it works
Record: Press the main action button to start your voice input.
Process: The application processes the audio and extracts key information such as event titles, start/end times, and notes.
Action: The extracted data is displayed in an organized dashboard for easy review.
Usage
Meeting entry: Speak the meeting title and time (e.g., "Marketing team meeting at 9:35 AM").
Task entry: Speak the task and deadline (e.g., "Prepare presentation due at 9:35 AM").
Notes: Add quick notes (e.g., "Review the contract after the meeting").
Note on Development
This application is currently in its prototype phase. It is designed to demonstrate the feasibility of converting natural language voice commands into structured data formats for personal productivity.
